# Google Veo Prompting Tips (from AI Video Communities)

To get the best results from Google Veo (the engine behind Google Vids), use these advanced prompting techniques:

### 1. The "Cinematic" Formula
`[Subject] + [Action] + [Environment] + [Lighting] + [Camera Movement] + [Style/Lens]`

*Example*: "A beautiful woman in a traditional Kazakh dress (sen sulu style) walking through a blooming apple orchard in Almaty, soft golden hour sunlight, slow motion cinematic tracking shot, 35mm lens, photorealistic."

### 2. Camera Movement Keywords
- **Static**: "Fixed camera", "Tripod shot".
- **Dynamic**: "Slow pan right", "Cinematic tilt up", "Drone flyover", "Handheld follow shot".
- **Zoom**: "Slow dolly zoom", "Smooth zoom in on face".

### 3. Lighting & Atmosphere
- "Volumetric lighting", "Soft rim light", "Natural sunlight through leaves".
- "Moody cinematic shadows", "High contrast", "Vibrant color grading".

### 4. Technical Specs (Veo understands these)
- "4k resolution", "Highly detailed textures", "Film grain", "24fps".
- "Depth of field", "Bokeh background".

### 5. Recreating "Sen Sulu" (Beauty Style)
For beauty/fashion videos like your reference:
- Focus on **skin textures**: "Flawless skin detail", "Soft focus background".
- Focus on **fabric**: "Flowing silk", "Intricate embroidery details".
- Focus on **slow motion**: "Slow motion 60fps" (makes it look more expensive).

### 6. What to avoid
- Don't use too many abstract words ("amazing", "beautiful" - be specific instead).
- Avoid complex multi-character interactions (Veo is better at 1-2 subjects).
