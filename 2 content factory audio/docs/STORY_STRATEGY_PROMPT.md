# Misha Timochko Storytelling Method (Hook-Context-Value-CTA)

This strategy is designed to maximize engagement and conversion in Instagram Stories by following a proven psychological sequence.

## 1. The Hook (Крючок) - 1-2 Stories
**Goal**: Stop the scroll. Make them curious.
- **Visual**: High-contrast, shocking, or very aesthetic. Use a "Close-up" or "Extreme Wide" shot.
- **Text**: Short, bold headline. "I didn't expect this...", "The biggest mistake in AI...", "How I made $10k in 2 days".
- **Nano Banana Tip**: Use a clean layout with one large, readable text block.

## 2. Context (Контекст) - 2-3 Stories
**Goal**: Build relatability and explain "Why now?".
- **Visual**: "Behind the scenes", "Talking head" (Veo), or a photo of a workspace/lifestyle.
- **Text**: Storytelling. "Yesterday I was thinking about...", "Many of you asked how I...", "The reality of building an AI agency is...".
- **Nano Banana Tip**: Use "Polaroid" or "Split screen" layouts to show process.

## 3. Value (Польза/Ценность) - 3-5 Stories
**Goal**: Provide actual insights. Make them save the story.
- **Visual**: Lists, diagrams, step-by-step screenshots, or cinematic b-roll (Veo).
- **Text**: "Step 1: ...", "3 tools I use...", "The secret formula is...".
- **Nano Banana Tip**: Use "List" or "Infographic" styles. Ensure text is perfectly aligned.

## 4. CTA (Призыв к действию) - 1-2 Stories
**Goal**: Convert the attention into action.
- **Visual**: Direct look into camera or a clear "Click here" visual.
- **Text**: "Link in bio", "Reply 'AI' to get the guide", "Join the waitlist".
- **Nano Banana Tip**: Use a "Button" style overlay or a clear arrow pointing to the bottom right.

## Visual Guidelines for AI Generation

### Nano Banana (Static Layouts)
- **Prompt Formula**: `[Layout Type] + [Subject] + [Text Content] + [Color Palette] + [Style]`
- **Example**: "Minimalist Instagram Story layout, a professional workspace with a laptop, text overlay saying 'AI for Business', beige and dark grey tones, high-end editorial style."

### Google Veo (Cinematic Video)
- **Prompt Formula**: `[Subject] + [Action] + [Environment] + [Lighting] + [Camera Movement] + [Style]`
- **Example**: "Cinematic slow pan of a modern office at sunset, dust particles dancing in the light, 4k, photorealistic, professional color grading."
